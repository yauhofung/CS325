Opcode | Symbol  | Description
00     | END     | Terminate program.
10     | LOAD    | Transfer contents from M(X) to AC.
11     | SAVE    | Transfer contents from AC to M(X).
20     | ADD     | Add M(X) by M(Y) and store result in AC.
21     | SUB     | Subtract M(X) by M(Y) and store result in AC.
22     | MUL     | Multiply M(X) by M(Y) and store result in AC.
23     | DIV     | Divide M(X) by M(Y) and store result in AC.
30     | GO_TO_X | Take next instruction from M(X).
31     | GO_TO_Y | Take next instruction from M(Y).
40     | GO_TO   | If AC is even, take next instruction from M(X). Otherwise, take next instruction from M(Y).
