Opcode | Description
00     | Terminate program.
10     | Operation failed.
11     | Operation succeeded.
20     | Read from M(X) to accumulator.
21     | Write from accumulator to M(X).
30     | Add M(X) by M(Y) and store result in accumulator.
31     | Subtract M(X) by M(Y) and store result in accumulator.
32     | Multiply M(X) by M(Y) and store result in accumulator.
33     | Divide M(X) by M(Y) and store result in accumulator.
40     | Take next instruction from M(X).
41     | Take next instruction from M(Y).
50     | If accumulator is even, take next instruction from M(X). Otherwise, take next instruction from M(Y).
